# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: May 30, 2026**

Dostok ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
We collect information you voluntarily provide when using the App:

**Account Information:**
- Email address (for account creation and communication)
- Display name
- Profile picture (optional)
- Language preferences

**Content You Create:**
- Chat messages and conversations with the AI
- Voice recordings during calls
- Notes and saved vocabulary
- Feedback and support requests

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically
We automatically collect certain information when you use the App:

**Device Information:**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- Screen resolution and device settings

**Usage Data:**
- Features used and frequency
- Session duration and timestamps
- Lesson completion and progress
- Error logs and crash reports

**Network Information:**
- IP address (anonymized after 30 days)
- Connection type (WiFi/cellular)
- General location (country/region level only)

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties
We may receive information from:
- **Google Play Store:** Purchase and subscription status
- **Firebase Analytics:** Anonymized usage patterns
- **Crashlytics:** App performance and crash data

- Provide and maintain the App
- Process your conversations with AI
- Deliver personalized learning content
- Manage your account and subscriptions

### 2.2 AI Improvement
- Train and improve our AI language models
- Enhance Darija language understanding

### 2.3 Analytics and Personalization
- Analyze usage patterns to improve the App
- Personalize your learning experience
- Track progress and provide recommendations
- Generate anonymized usage statistics

### 2.4 Communication
- Send account-related notifications
- Respond to support requests
- Send optional marketing communications (with consent)
- Notify you of App updates

### 2.5 Legal and Safety
- Comply with legal obligations
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Protect user safety

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 3.1 We Do NOT Share Data for Marketing
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

### 3.2 Service Providers
We may share data with trusted service providers who assist in operating the App:
- **Google Cloud/Firebase:** Hosting, analytics, and infrastructure
- **Google Play Store:** Payment processing
- **Analytics Services:** Anonymized usage data only

All service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

### 3.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:
- Valid legal process (subpoena, court order)
- Government investigations
- Protection of our rights or safety
- Prevention of illegal activities

### 3.4 Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction. You will be notified of any such change.

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Encryption at rest for sensitive data
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication
- Secure data centers

### 4.2 Data Retention
- **Active accounts:** Data retained while account is active
- **Deleted accounts:** Data permanently deleted within 90 days
- **Anonymized data:** May be retained indefinitely for analytics
- **Legal requirements:** Some data retained as required by law

### 4.3 Breach Notification
In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will:
- Notify you within 72 hours
- Provide details of the breach
- Offer guidance on protective measures
- Report to relevant authorities as required

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access and Portability
You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Export your data in a machine-readable format
- Receive a copy of your data

**To request your data:**
- Email: privacy@dostok.app
- In-app: Settings > Privacy > Export My Data

### 5.2 Correction
You can update or correct your information:
- In-app: Settings > Profile > Edit
- Email: privacy@dostok.app

### 5.3 Deletion
You can request deletion of your account and data:
- In-app: Settings > Account > Delete Account
- Email: privacy@dostok.app
- Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone

### 5.4 Opt-Out Options
You can opt out of:
- **Marketing emails:** Unsubscribe link in emails
- **Push notifications:** Device settings
- **Analytics:** Settings > Privacy > Analytics
- **Personalization:** Settings > Privacy > Personalization

### 5.5 Restrict Processing
You can request that we restrict processing of your data under certain circumstances.
